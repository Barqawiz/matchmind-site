How do AI explanations work?
Tap any goal, card, penalty or VAR moment in a match, and Kickwise generates a clear, instant explanation of what happened and why it matters.
How is the leaderboard fair?
Only predictions you make before kickoff count toward your Game Center rank. You can still predict live matches and share them, but those don't affect the ranked board.
What does Kickwise Pro include?
Unlimited AI insights, every match, team briefings and post-match debriefs. It starts with a free trial, then renews monthly. You can cancel anytime.
How do I manage or cancel my subscription?
On your iPhone: Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions → Kickwise, then change or cancel. Cancelling stops future renewals; you keep Pro until the period ends.
How do I restore a purchase?
Open Kickwise, go to the Pro screen and tap Restore. Make sure you're signed in with the Apple ID used for the purchase.
Do I need an account?
No. Kickwise works without sign-up. Game Center (for the leaderboard) is optional.
Which languages are supported?
English, Español, Français, Português and العربية, with full right-to-left support for Arabic.
